Founded in 2022, Bichikat was born from a vision to redefine luxury fashion, where exclusivity, craftsmanship, and innovation come together to create something truly rare. From the very beginning, our philosophy has been clear: to produce exceptional pieces in strictly limited numbers, ensuring rarity and timeless appeal. Each collection is crafted in ultra-exclusive quantities, never restocked, never repeated. Every piece is designed to be rare, collectible, and enduring.
At Bichikat, quality isn’t just a standard; it’s an obsession. Our T-shirts are made from ultra-premium Kashmiri cotton, renowned for its exceptional softness, breathability, and durability. Unlike conventional cotton, this luxury-grade fabric ages beautifully, becoming even softer over time while maintaining its structure. Every garment undergoes precision stitching with a high-thread-count finish, ensuring longevity and impeccable detailing. The same commitment extends to our bespoke packaging, crafted from high-density recycled paper with a matte finish, offering a tactile, eco-conscious unboxing experience that reflects our dedication to excellence in every aspect.
Bichikat isn’t just about fashion; it’s about an experience. From the moment you receive your order, unboxing a Bichikat piece feels like owning a work of art. Every touchpoint, from the first glance to the final wear, is designed to be effortless, refined, and unforgettable.
When excellence is the standard, rarity is inevitable.
